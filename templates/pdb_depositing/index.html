This tool will help you collect information for depositing your PDB file into the Protein Data Bank

When you pressed the button, the information contained in the experiment and dataset was collected and inserted into the author-infor.text template. It is not yet complete, you will still need to add further information.

Below the box you can find a download link.

The more data you keep in mytardis, the more we will be able to prefill automatically.

Feedback on this feature is most welcome.
